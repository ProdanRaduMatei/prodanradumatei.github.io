CyclinGo

The CyclinGo project has a dual format, consisting of both a hardware component and a mobile application. The hardware part is represented by an intelligent signaling system for cyclists in the form of a LED vest. Through the mobile application, the vest can be personalized and used as a platform for promoting brands that wish to display their logos in traffic.
